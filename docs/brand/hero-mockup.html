The Manual 01 — A different kind of inbox

Your inbox is by invitation.
Everything else expires.

ZeroSpam is whitelist-first email. We default-deny so you don't have to.

New senders land in a screener. You decide who gets in. Anyone you haven't waved through goes to quarantine, which clears itself on a schedule you set. No filters guessing at intent. No backlog. No inbox debt. // the only score is the one you keep.

Footnote 01 — The Screener

A new sender shows up. You decide.

Yes lets them through. No mutes them for thirty days. That's the entire interaction model. No swipes, no scores, no machine learning.

Footnote 02 — Quarantine

Mail that clears itself.

Anything not whitelisted lands in quarantine and expires on a TTL you set — 168 hours by default. The backlog is finite. The TTL is yours to lower.

Footnote 03 — The List

You own the guest list.

One row, one rule. Address, domain, or regex. Edit it like you edit a contact card. No machine learning. No opaque scores.
