ZeroSpam is whitelist-first email. Senders you've allowed reach you. The rest land in quarantine and clear themselves out on a schedule you set. No filters guessing at intent. No inbox debt.

Footnote 01

The screener.

A new sender shows up. Yes lets them through. No mutes them for thirty days. That's the entire interaction model.

Footnote 02

Quarantine that expires.

Anything not whitelisted lands here and clears itself on a TTL you set. 168 hours by default. No backlog, ever.

Footnote 03

You own the guest list.

One row, one rule. Address, domain, or regex. Edit it like you edit a contact card. No machine learning, no opaque scores.
